# Bedtime Stories

A personalized bedtime story generator for kids with a persistent hero, story universe continuity, and nightly resume — your child's own story world that grows night after night.

## Trigger Phrases

- `"bedtime story"`
- `"tell me a story"`
- `"story time"`
- `"read me a story"`
- `"continue my story"`
- `"new story"` / `"new adventure"`
- `"story please"`

## How It Works

**First time:** The ability asks three quick questions — the child's name, age, and favourite animal or creature. It silently generates a personalised hero and story universe (e.g. "Emmaly, a curious explorer who loves foxes, in the Moonveil Forest where animals can speak"). The first story begins immediately after.

**Every night after:** Says where the hero left off last night and continues the story, or starts a fresh adventure in the same world — referencing past events.

**During the story:** For ages 6+, the story pauses once for a simple binary choice ("Should Emmaly follow the butterfly or cross the bridge?"). The child answers, and the story continues based on their choice. If the child doesn't respond, the story finds a peaceful ending on its own.

**Story length:**
- Ages 3–5: 2 short segments, no choices (purely passive listening)
- Ages 6+: 3 segments, 1 choice in the middle

## What Makes It Different from Alexa

| | Alexa | Bedtime Stories |
|---|---|---|
| Story variety | Fixed library | Infinite, LLM-generated |
| Personalisation | None | Child's name, age, favourites woven in |
| Resume | None | Resumes from exact narrative checkpoint |
| Character continuity | None | Same hero evolves across nights |
| Interactive choice | None | 1 binary choice per story (ages 6+) |
| Quiet child | Awkward | Auto-closes story gracefully |

## Setup

No API keys required. All stories are generated by the built-in LLM.

Child profile is saved automatically after the first session and persists across nights.

## Story Data

Stored under the key `bedtime_story_data`:

- `child_name`, `child_age`, `hero_name`, `hero_description`, `universe`
- `current_story` — active story checkpoint and choices made
- `story_history` — summaries of past completed stories (last 10)

To reset and start fresh, ask the assistant to clear the ability's data or delete the `bedtime_story_data` key.

## Example Session

**First time:**

> User: "Bedtime story"
> AI: "Hi! I'm your personal storyteller. What's your name?"
> Child: "Emma"
> AI: "Hi Emma! How old are you?"
> Child: "Seven"
> AI: "And Emma, what's your favourite animal or magical creature?"
> Child: "Foxes"
> AI: "Perfect! Your hero is Emmaly, a curious explorer who carries a silver compass and loves foxes. Get cozy, Emma. Your first adventure starts now."
> *(story begins)*

**Returning night:**

> User: "Continue my story"
> AI: "Welcome back, Emma! Last night, Emmaly had just found a glowing door hidden behind the oldest oak tree. Let's see what's inside."
> *(story resumes from checkpoint)*

**Mid-story choice:**

> AI: "...and there, at the edge of the clearing, Emmaly saw two paths. One led up to a sparkling cave, the other wound down to a cozy village. Should Emmaly go to the cave, or head to the village?"
> Child: "The cave!"
> AI: *(continues story with cave path)*

**Story end:**

> AI: "...and as the moon rose above the Moonveil Forest, Emmaly smiled and closed her eyes, safe and warm. The end. Sweet dreams, Emma. Emmaly's next adventure will be waiting for you tomorrow night."
