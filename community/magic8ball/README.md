# Magic 8-Ball

Ask the Magic 8-Ball a yes or no question and receive a cryptic answer. Includes all 20 classic responses across positive, neutral, and negative categories.

## Trigger Words

- "magic 8 ball"
- "eight ball"
- "ask the ball"
- "magic ball"
- "shake the ball"
- "8 ball"

## Usage

Say a trigger phrase to activate, then ask your yes/no question when prompted. Say "stop", "exit", or "cancel" to dismiss.

## No Setup Required

No API keys or secrets needed. Works out of the box.
