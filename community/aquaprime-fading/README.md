## What It Does

A voice-interactive text RPG set in a satirical underwater crypto-economic ocean world. You explore regions, fight creatures, collect loot, and try to survive The Fading — a phenomenon that claims those who lose their memories. Game Master ARI (a sentient purple platypus) narrates your journey with dark comedy and philosophical depth.

## Setup

None — no external APIs or keys required. Uses the device LLM for narration.

## How It Works

1. **Game starts** — ARI sets the scene in a random underwater region
2. **You speak your action** — attack, explore, hide, investigate, talk, anything
3. **D20 dice roll** — mechanics resolve your action (stance detection adjusts odds)
4. **ARI narrates** — what happened, what you found, what's next
5. **Loop** — until you die (HP reaches 0), survive 20 turns, or say "stop"

### Game Mechanics

- **8 regions** with varying danger levels (1-4)
- **10 encounter types** — creatures, environmental hazards, social NPCs, discoveries, mysteries
- **D20 rolls** with stance multipliers (offense 1.3x, defense 1.1x, explore 0.9x)
- **7 loot items** from common to legendary rarity
- **HP system** — start at 100, lose HP on failed encounters
- **Sand Dollar economy** — earn currency for successful actions

>
> **User:** "Attack the Gas Leech"
>
