---

## How It Happens

1. The OpenHome team monitors high-performing community Abilities
2. We reach out to the author to discuss promotion
3. We do a deeper code review and may suggest changes
4. Once approved, the Ability moves to `official/`
5. Author is credited in the README, CONTRIBUTORS.md, and release notes
6. OpenHome takes over maintenance going forward

---

## What the Author Gets

- Permanent credit as the original creator
- Recognition in CONTRIBUTORS.md
- Mention in release notes
- Their Ability reaching more users via featured Marketplace placement

---

## Can I Request Promotion?

Yes. Open an issue with the title `[Promotion Request] your-ability-name` and we'll review it. Meeting the thresholds above doesn't guarantee promotion, but it puts you in strong consideration.
