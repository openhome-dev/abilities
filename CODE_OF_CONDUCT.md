# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful.** Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. Constructive criticism is welcome; personal attacks are not.

**Be collaborative.** We're building something together. Help others, share knowledge, and give credit where it's due.

**Be patient.** Not everyone has the same experience level. Welcome newcomers and help them get started.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to the OpenHome team. All reports will be reviewed and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.
